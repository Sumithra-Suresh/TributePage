Periyar E V Ramasamy

Father of Dravidians

Here's a time line of E.V.R:

  • 1879 - Born in Erode, Chennai, India
  • At a young age, he began questioning the apparent contradictions in the Hindu mythological stories. As Ramasamy grew, he felt that people used religion only as a mask to deceive innocent people and therefore took it as one of his duties in life to warn people against superstitions and priests.
  • (1919–1925) - Member of Congress Party
  • (1924–1925) - Vaikom Satyagraha
  • (1929–1932) - International travel
  • 1937 - Opposition to Hindi
  • (1938–1944) - President of the Justice Party
  • (1944–onwards) - Dravidar Kazhagam
  • 1973 - died at the age of 94.
Anti-caste reformer and Iconic thinker of India!
